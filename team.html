Team

3scale provides an API management platform as well as API strategy. Our team is passionate about APIs (application programming interfaces) and we love to build things, so we got together to build an API management solution to power the programmable web. Based in San Francisco, California, and Barcelona, Spain, the 3scale team is committed to providing high-value, cost-effective solutions for API and Web Services providers.

Steven Willmott (Ph.D.)

Co-founder and CEO
steve@3scale.net

Steven was previously the research director of one of the leading European research groups in Europe on distributed systems and Artificial Intelligence at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya in Barcelona, Spain. He brings 10 years of technical experience in Web Services, Semantic Web, network technology and the management of large-scale international R&D teams.

Mark Cheshire

COO
mark@3scale.net

Mark was previously VP Products at NTRGlobal group, where he was responsible for product management, portfolio planning, and usability engineering for NTRglobal's SaaS solutions. Before that he was at HP Software as global head of Product Management for the IT Management business unit, with responsibilities for P&L, product strategy, and go-to-market. Mark holds a degree in Software Engineering from the University Of Birmingham, UK and an MBA from MIT's Sloan School of Management, Cambridge, MA.

Andrew Mackenzie

VP Engineering
andrew@3scale.net

Andrew's recent experience includes being RD manager for Hewlett-Packard's Forms Automation Solution as well as Entrepreneur and Principle Engineer in the Social Networking and Telecoms space. He brings 23 years of experience in engineering and management of product development in global, multinational environments. Andrew counts IP creation and management, software quality and managing diverse, geographically spread teams among his specialties.

Trisha Ristagno

Marketing Director
trisha@3scale.net

An established tech marketing pro with start-up and blue chip experience with companies ranging from Splunk to IBM and Microsoft, Trisha has been described as a strategist who gets things done. In both in-house and agency environments she has led and managed detailed, high-level strategic projects as well as large, long-term initiatives involving many stakeholders and moving parts. Her specialities include branding and positioning, building strong digital marketing organizations, managing diverse teams, and finding connections between seemingly disparate things.
