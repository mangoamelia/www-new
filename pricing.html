Frequently Asked Questions

How long are the contracts and how do I cancel service?

Unless you sign up for our yearly plan, all 3scale products are month to month. We make it simple to start – and stop – your service at any time. You can cancel the service by simply sending an email to sales@3scale.net. Canceling 3scale is an instantaneous and no-questions-asked process.

Is there a discount for yearly service?

Yes, we offer a 10% discount on up-front yearly commitments. There are no refunds on yearly service contracts. If you're interested in purchasing a full-year plan, please contact us.

How do I integrate with 3scale?

Integrating your API with 3scale's back-end in order to have your API Management service up and running is fast and easy: simply download the appropriate plug-in or use the 3scale Service Management API or and follow the instructions. You can also choose to use APIcast, 3scale's cloud-hosted API gateway, for free for up to 50,000 transactions per month.

To learn more about integration options, see our Quickstart support article.

How do I know if I need to upgrade to the next plan level?

3scale will notify you by email each time your traffic volume overreaches its current contractual upper limit. We understand that sometimes testing and other factors lead to brief spikes in traffic, so you won't be forced to upgrade if this only happens once. If you exceed your limit more than 3 times per week for any 2 weeks of the plan month, a member of our sales team will contact you to upgrade your account.
